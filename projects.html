Academic mini Project--Amusement park Booking


Amusement Park ticket booking is an innovative website for booking tickets to enjoy different rides in an amusement park. In this website admin includes the details of different rides and user can select their suitable rides according to them. Most of the rides have age limitations, so user have to choose rides based on their age.


Academic main Project-E-Ration Management System

Departing from traditional manual methods fraught with inefficiencies, E-Ration automates critical tasks, streamlining operations while fortifying system integrity.
